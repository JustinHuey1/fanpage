Soren Bjerg was born in Denmark in 1997. He first came into the Eu league scene in 2013 and played for CopenHagen Wolves. Later that year, he was transported to NA to be on the team TSM as their mid laner. After joining TSM, he has been very successful and has been to every NALCS finals(except for this year) and World Championship. Bjergsen is considered to be one of the best in NA and ranked very high across the world.
